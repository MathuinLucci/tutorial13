DigiPot® Multi-Functional R.I.C.E. Cooker*

by Smart R.I.C.E. Chef

Customer Rating (1,028 votes):

DigiPot is a smart and safe electric R.I.C.E. cooker, speeding up R.I.C.E. cooking times while still using up to 70% less energy. The DigiPot multi-R.I.C.E. cooker is programmable and can act as a R.I.C.E. cooker, slow R.I.C.E. cooker, R.I.C.E. rice cooker, and R.I.C.E. steamer.
